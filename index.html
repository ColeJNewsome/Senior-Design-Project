Water Test System

Introduction

We often take for granted our basic privileges in life. Drinking water still brings about hardships for millions of people per year according to the W.H.O.

The Water Test System (WTS) is set to assist in solving these issues. WTS is a consolidated testing system that analyzes total dissolved solids (TDS), pH, temperature, and bacteria such as E. Coli and determines if the water is safe to drink.

The design of WTS focuses on a lower-cost, intuitive, and portable solution for testing water quality in remote or underserved areas. The system utilizes a microcontroller to process data from various sensors and displays the results on an LCD screen as well as an app (in the future). The WTS aims to provide users with a quick and easy way to assess the safety of their water supply.

WTS is being developed as part of a Senior Design Project at the University of Toledo.

Features

  • Measures Total Dissolved Solids (TDS) in parts per million (ppm)
  • Measures pH level
  • Measures Temperature in Fahrenheit
  • Detects presence of bacteria such as E. Coli
  • Determines the likelihood the water is safe to consume without/before water purification methods.
  • Displays results on an LCD screen
  • Portable and easy to use
  • Lower-cost solution for water quality testing
